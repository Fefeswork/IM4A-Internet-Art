Fonts and colours:

There isn’t a fully established colour palette for this website. The colour palette has changed based on the backgrounds I’ve used: the current colour consists of light blue, light grey, and some hints of black and white. This colour palette is nice and simple, and it isn’t too harsh on the eyes. I do plan on changing the colour palette again just to make the website feel gamier. In terms of the font: I’ve decided to work with two different fonts, those being Times New Roman and Arial: both these fonts are easy to read and provide accessibility for people with reading impairments.
